The Proposal

On Saturday 9th July 2016, we got engaged in front of 50 of our closest friends and a load of random strangers in Primrose Hill, North London. It was a special day, and one that we’ll never forget. It even made the Daily Mail, but we won’t link to the article because you’ll end up with their website in your browser history and no one wants that.

Having met ten years ago in student halls and sharing numerous flats with friends since, we’ve spent a lot of our relationship surrounded by some really amazing people. Those people have been so important to both of us and that's the reason they were the ones to pop the question. A massive thank you to everyone who made it happen.

The Ceremony

Saturday 16th September 2017
Arrive for 11:30am (ceremony at 12pm)

Holy Trinity Church
Old Wolverton Rd
Wolverton
Milton Keynes
MK12 5NJ

Directions

Nearest train station: Wolverton (trains are faster and more frequent to Milton Keynes Central)

From south or north via M1

The journey is about 7 miles and shouldn’t take more than 15 minutes or so. At Junction 14, take exit to A509 Newport Pagnell. After 1 mile, take the first exit at Tickford roundabout. After ½ mile, take the second exit at Marsh End Roundabout onto A422 Monks Way dual carriageway.

Stay on A422 Monks Way dual carriageway for approx. 2 miles, via 4 roundabouts (this is Milton Keynes…) then at the 5th roundabout take the 3rd exit onto Grafton St V6 towards Wolverton. At the next roundabout take the second exit, remaining on Grafton St V6.

After approx. ½ mile at next roundabout take second exit, still Grafton St , signposted Old Wolverton industrial area. Very quickly you’ll come to another roundabout. Take the first exit onto Old Wolverton Rd under a railway bridge.

Follow the road for approx. 1 mile. Look out for the Galleon pub on your right as you cross the Grand Union canal. Within 200 yards the entrance to the church car park is on your right, immediately before the Slated Row school entrance on your left and opposite Slated Row. There is a brown Holy Trinity Church roadsign pointing to the entrance.

Getting from the church to the reception venue

Driving from the church to the reception venue takes approximately 30 minutes. Here's a map of the fastest route.

We will also be providing transport from the church to the venue. Please let us know if you will need transport when filling out the RSVP form below or by contacting us separately before 1st July 2017.

The Reception

Saturday 16th September 2017
From 6pm until midnight

Poundon House
Poundon, Nr. Bicester
OX27 9BB

Directions

Nearest train station: Bicester North

From London

Take junction 9 off the M40. At the roundabout above the motorway, head towards Bicester on the A41. At the Esso petrol station roundabout, turn right. You are now on the Bicester bypass.

At the next roundabout, take the second left towards Launton. Follow signs to Launton. In Launton, at the crossroads, turn left opposite The Bull Inn, this should be marked Poundon.

Leave the village, going over a railway track about a mile out, and then go straight over the next cross roads. About 3 miles from there, after going up a steep hill and past a couple of farm entrances, we are on your right with black gates and a lime tree avenue.

From Buckingham

On the Buckingham / Brackley ringroad, follow signs to Gawcott. Continue into Gawcott and turn left at the T-junction, signed towards Marsh Gibbon / Twyford. Continue until you past the Seven Stars pub on your right, where you then take the next right to Twyford.

Turn right into Twyford and continue through the village. About 1 mile out of Twyford, turn right towards Poundon, then take the left by the Sow and Pigs Pub. Drive up the village.

Poundon House is the last house on the left with the black iron gates and limetree avenue.
