Silent
2012 — Oil on Board

Frosty snow capped mountains make up this staggered and splintered pastel landscape. Contrasted conditions are visible in weather and atmosphere - presence of ice and glaciers cut through by a stroke of melting orange magma. It is the world in limbo. This piece displays a distressing and challenging climate, referencing current climate change and the condition of our planet today.
