Begins

2012 — Oil on Board

Calm and serene blues and greys make up what appears to be floating worlds within a congregation of rocks, water and cavernous holes beneath surfaces of land and sea. Glowing lights from within the caves lay way to a mystic and yet grounding point of reference to the resilience and determination of the human race - creation of life itself being cyclical and everlasting. Worlds buried or submerged, can be seen humming with the sounds and smells of mankind.
