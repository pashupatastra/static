An Odyssey

2012 — Oil on Board

Aztec temples and Atlantan cities dwell beneath the peak of the mountain. Tension is heavy in the sky. The perfect storm is forming above. Contrast is displayed between the delicately executed underwater world of the deep and the rough with the quick, fickle nature of the brush strokes in the sky. The intention and considered use of Mayan civilisation could be a reference to theories surrounding a 2012 apocalypse.
