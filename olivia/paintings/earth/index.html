Earth
2012 — Oil on Board

This circular painting is heavy with dramatic references to the Book of Revelations. Its visuals articulate a destructive and yet beautiful happening, managing to instil a weary and fearful tone coupled with an overpowering appreciation of the mysteries and wonders of mother nature.
