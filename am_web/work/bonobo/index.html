My submission for the D&AD Student Awards 2011, nominated for an award in the illustration category. The brief set by Diesel was to 'create an illustrated interpretation of a musical track that is experienced by the viewer in an unconventional and pioneering way.' The product is a 7" record sleeve with a difference. Three square panels fit into a cut-out shape on one side. The other three fit into the same shape, inverted on the other side. These panels accompany the record with artwork on the inner circle.
